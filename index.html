Data

The microclimate sensor readings and locations are used for the first part of the analysis. This part focuses on studying temporal development of the microclimate of certain areas of Melbourne.

The pedestrian counting sensor locations and readings are used for the social aspect of the analysis. Merging these two datasets will give us an insight on how this social aspect is affecting certain microclimate environments.
